VISA INFO
Any foreign visitors wishing to enter the Republic of Korea must have a valid passport and obtain a Korean visa before coming. But most foreigners (in 99 countries) who want to visit Korea just for a short-term tour or transit are permitted to enter Korea without a visa according to the principles of reciprocity or priority of national interest with a tourist/transit visa status (B-2, 30 days). For more detailed information, please click here. To obtain a visa to attend this conference, some embassies will request you to present an invitation letter. We can only provide assistance letters for attendees who meet one or more of the following requirements:
  • Have submitted a technical paper for review
  • Are the author/presenter of an accepted technical paper, panel, workshop or demo session
  • Have registered for the conference with payment
To request a visa invitation letter, email Seoyeon Kang with information about the above requirements.
We do not issue letters of invitation for employees of commercial organizations from countries that require a visa to Korea unless the company is known for being active in network measurement, analysis and related fields. If in doubt, please contact us before registering.
